About Us 

              
         Manufacturing magnate Italian Ferruccio Lamborghini founded the company in 1963 .

         The company's first models, such as the 350 GT, were released in the mid-1960s.

         Lamborghini was noted for the 1966 Miura sports coupé, which used a rear mid-engine,

         rear-wheel drive layout.Lamborghini grew rapidly during its first ten years, but sales

         fell in the wake of the 1973 worldwide financial downturn and the oil crisis.

         The company went bankrupt in 1978, and was placed in the receivership of brothers 

         Jean-Claude and Patrick Mimran in 1980.....read more
